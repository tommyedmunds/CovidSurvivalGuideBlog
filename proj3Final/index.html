Covid And Us

The Toll of Covid-19 on Young Americans

Millions of young people face an unprecedented crisis. In the face of this, people need guidance on how to handle this new reality.

As millions of young people graduate high school and college and prepare to enter the workforce, many face increasing senses of uncertainty, even as vaccination numbers grow.
